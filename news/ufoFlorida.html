Unidentified Flying Object Spotted Hovering Over Miami Beach

Early morning beachgoers in Miami were baffled this week when they spotted what appears to be a legitimate unidentified flying object over the ocean. Witnesses describe seeing a large silverish orb hovering silently above the water for several minutes before zooming away at remarkable speed.

The purported UFO was captured clearly on cell phone cameras by multiple people who observed it. Local police and aviation authorities say they cannot account for the unexplained object caught on video based on known aircraft in the area. The photos and videos have now sparked intense debate and speculation among UFO enthusiasts.

Explanations for the Miami Beach sighting run the gamut from balloons to meteors to advanced military craft. But with no persuasive identification, many continue pressing authorities to further investigate the legitimacy of an otherworldly visitation. Miami now joins a long list of global cities where unexplained aerial objects have piqued public imagination about the possibility of extraterrestrial tourists frequently passing by Earth undetected.
