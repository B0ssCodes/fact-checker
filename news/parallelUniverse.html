Scientists Propose Existence of Parallel Universe Where Time Runs Backwards

A bold new theory developed by physicists suggests our universe may be accompanied by a parallel universe where time runs in reverse. The controversial idea proposes that a companion universe could exist alongside our own with inverted spacetime where causes precede effects.

The hypothetical model challenges long accepted concepts of linear time. Its architects say mathematical calculations support the possibility of a mirrored universe with opposite time orientation. Skeptics argue there is insufficient physical evidence to validate the radical idea.

Still, the notion of cosmic twins where time flows backwards holds fascinating philosophical implications. Studies to further develop the model are ongoing but could fundamentally reshape our comprehension of time, space and cosmic evolution. For now, the “anti-universe” where history unfolds in reverse remains firmly in the realm of theory and imagination.
